Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your TopBooks account and associated data, you can do so directly within the application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the TopBooks application.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion.

Once the account is deleted, all associated user data stored through Firebase services will be permanently removed.

Data deleted

  • User profile information
  • Saved books and preferences
  • Application usage data associated with the account

Contact

If you cannot access the app and need assistance deleting your account, please contact the developer.
